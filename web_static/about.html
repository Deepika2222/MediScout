About MediScout

Our Mission

MediScout aims to bridge the gap between patients and pharmacies. We provide real-time information on medicine availability and offer intelligent symptom-based suggestions to help you manage minor health issues effectively.

Disclaimer

MediScout is an informational tool and does not provide medical advice. The suggestions generated are based on general data and should not replace professional medical consultation. Always visit a doctor for serious symptoms or emergencies.
